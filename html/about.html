About the USS Enterprise

The USS Enterprise was commissioned in 1937. She was the first aircraft carrier on the scene after the Attack on Pearl Harbor. She then went on to serve in nearly every major battle in the Pacific during World War II, earning the most decorations out of any ship in that conflict. She helped change the course of the war in the Pacific. She was decommissioned in 1947 and then scrapped eleven years later after efforts to preserve her were unsuccessful. She now only lives on in artifacts and memories.
